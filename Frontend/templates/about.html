About VitalGaze

Why We Chose This Project?

Our growing system project began with the realization that the world of AI is still missing effective health monitoring solutions in workplace and educational institutions. With the up-rising concerns about individuals unknowingly being under the influence of substances or experiencing untreated health issues, we saw an opportunity to leverage cutting-edge technology to foster safer environments. Motivated by the potential to provide a proactive solution, reinforced by the profound impact on public safety and well-being, we embarked on this project to develop a system that can offer real-time insights and early warnings, ultimately contributing to a safer and healthier environment.
